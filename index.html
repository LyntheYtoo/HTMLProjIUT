WELCOME TO TASHKENT!

Welcome to the eclectic and bustling city of Tashkent, one of the main political and transportation hubs of Central Asia.

Tashkent is a wonderful mixture of a modern metropolis and the unique colors of an eastern city.
Taking a walk in Tashkent, you will see graceful European constructions, monumental buildings of the Soviet era, blue domes of mosques and madrasas, and glittering facades of modern buildings.
